BitterSweet Thesis

"Toothache"
Children are commonly told that too much candy and sugar causes cavities. While sugar promotes the creation of acid which breaks down teeth,
acid breakdown is the reason for cavities. Sugar is not the only food that promotes acid;
food and drinks such as citrus and wine also promote acid breakdown.
